About

With 15+ years of global IT leadership, I am a Certified SAP Master Data Governance Architect with a proven record of delivering innovative, enterprise-scale solutions across pharmaceuticals, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, and beyond. My core focus lies in Master Data Governance (MDG), data integration, quality, privacy, cybersecurity, and SAP archiving.

I bring deep expertise in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML)—developing AI-driven data pipelines, governance automation, and predictive analytics frameworks tailored for life sciences and regulated industries. My published research explores how AI/ML can reshape enterprise data management and compliance.

As a recognized thought leader, I serve as an international speaker, conference session chair, and judge for global innovation platforms like the Edison Awards and IEEE events. I regularly contribute to the AI and MDM communities through scholarly publications, technical articles, and peer reviews.

My work emphasizes secure data architecture—incorporating cybersecurity protocols and compliance frameworks (HIPAA, GDPR, MDR) into every stage of the data lifecycle. I’ve led product development initiatives, global rollouts, and SAP MDG transformations across 80+ countries.
